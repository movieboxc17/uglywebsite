Fascinating Facts About Electronics and Computers:

Did you know that:

  • 1. The first computer bug was a real insect - a moth trapped inside a computer, which inspired the term "bug" in computer programming.
  • 2. The world's first electronic digital computer, ENIAC, weighed over 27 tons and occupied a large room.
  • 3. The term "byte" was coined by Dr. Werner Buchholz in 1956 to represent a group of binary digits, typically 8 bits.
  • 4. The first computer mouse was made of wood and had two wheels.
  • 5. The Apollo 11 mission's guidance computer had less processing power than a modern smartphone.
  • 6. Computers use billions of transistors, which are tiny electronic switches, to perform calculations.
  • 7. The first electronic digital computer game, "Spacewar!," was created in 1962 by Steve Russell and became the precursor to modern video games.
  • 8. The concept of a computer "virus" was coined by Frederick Cohen in 1983 to describe a self-replicating program.
  • 9. The World Wide Web (WWW) was invented by Sir Tim Berners-Lee in 1989 while working at CERN.
  • 10. The first webcam was used to monitor a coffee pot in the computer science department at Cambridge University in 1991.
