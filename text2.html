Fascinating Facts About Computer Viruses and Malware:

  • 1. The first computer virus, known as the "Creeper" virus, was developed in the early 1970s and ran on the TENEX operating system.
  • 2. The term "malware" is a combination of "malicious" and "software" and refers to any software designed to harm or exploit computer systems.
  • 3. The "ILOVEYOU" virus in 2000 was one of the most destructive email viruses, causing billions of dollars in damages worldwide.
  • 4. Antivirus software, such as Norton and McAfee, is designed to detect and remove viruses and malware from computer systems.
  • 5. Worms, Trojans, and ransomware are common types of malware, each with different methods and objectives.
